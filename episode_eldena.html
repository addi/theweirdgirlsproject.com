Special Episode: Eldena Ruins

In connection with the nordic arts festival Nordischer Klang in Greifswald Germany, Kitty was commissioned to produce an Episode to screen as the opening work. After researching the area Kitty chose to shoot in the ruined monastery Eldena Abbey – a historic part of the city which famously Caspar David Friedrich painted frequently.
