Episode 1: Neon Fame and The Last Supper

The first episode of The Weird Girls Project was somewhat of an experiment. At this stage there was no plan to make this into a series, it was concentrating on the event itself and the media produced was a record of the event. It was split into two aspects. The initial being a photographic interpretation of Leonardo da Vinci's painting The Last Supper. The second part of Episode 1: Neon Fame was a video piece recorded in the dark with only neon strips to accentuate movement.
