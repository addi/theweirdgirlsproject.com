Episode 6: Winter Mermaids

This took place in early October at Hveragerði’s Sundlaugin Laugaskarði and has produced a music film to accompany Emiliana Torrini’s single I Heard it All Before.

The video’s inspiration was 1930s director Busby Berekley and was directed by Ali Taylor of Academy Films. Ali won best video in the Icelandic Music awards 2005 for her Sunny Road video for Emiliana. This video went on to win the Music Video category at 2010's Northern Wave Film Festival.
