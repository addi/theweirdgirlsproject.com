Episode 14: Replica

American/Swedish solo artist Elodie (Elissa Björke) approached Kitty for a collaboration due to an interest in what The Weird Girls Project stood for. Contrasting the cinematic surroundings and contact with nature in the previous episode, Kitty worked together with Arnar Valdimarsson to utilize technology for the concept on Episode 14. Kitty had an ongoing intrigue with infinity mirrors and initially began investigating using actual mirrors for this piece. After researching it became apparent that video feedback would produce a similar effect. As a large proportion of the Project shows, Kitty has deep rooted attraction to repeat imagery and geometrical shapes - this video focuses purely on that concept.
